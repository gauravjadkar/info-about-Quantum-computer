Quantum computers are advanced computing systems that utilize the principles of quantum mechanics to process information. Unlike classical computers that use bits (0 or 1) for processing, quantum computers use quantum bits, or qubits, which can exist in multiple states simultaneously. This allows them to perform certain calculations much faster than traditional computers.
